Our Story

Built around one simple idea — everyone deserves fresh, affordable groceries

FreshMart opened its first store in 2014 with a single promise: stock the freshest produce, price it fairly, and treat every customer like a neighbor. That promise now powers 48+ branches, a loyalty program trusted by 320,000+ members, and weekly offers that put real savings back into your basket.

Today, our buyers work directly with local farms and trusted suppliers so we can pass the savings — and the freshness — straight to you, every single week.
