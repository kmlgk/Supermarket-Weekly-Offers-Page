Grocery bills creep up quietly — a few extra items here, a missed offer there. The good news: a handful of small, repeatable habits can meaningfully lower your weekly spend without sacrificing quality or convenience.

1. Plan around this week's offers, not your usual list

Instead of shopping from a fixed list, check FreshMart's weekly offers first and build your meals around what's discounted. Produce and meat offers rotate weekly, so flexibility is where the biggest savings hide.

2. Stack loyalty points with weekly discounts

Your FreshMart Loyalty Card earns points on every purchase — and those points stack directly on top of weekly offers. Silver and Gold members even get 24-hour early access before deals go public.

"The families who save the most aren't buying less — they're buying smarter, aligned with what's already on offer that week." — FreshMart Loyalty Team

3. Buy seasonal produce

Seasonal fruit and vegetables are cheaper and fresher. Our seasonal produce guide (linked below) is updated monthly so you always know what's at its best price.

4. Use click & collect to avoid impulse buys

Ordering online and collecting in-store keeps you to your list — no wandering aisles, no impulse extras at the checkout.

5. Batch cook with discounted meat and frozen goods

Buy meat when it's discounted and freeze portions for later in the week — this alone can cut a family's meat spend by 20-30%.

6. Bring reusable bags and check unit prices

FreshMart's shelf tags always show price-per-unit — use it to compare pack sizes rather than sticker price alone.

7. Set a weekly offers reminder

Subscribe to our newsletter or enable app notifications so you never miss Monday's fresh batch of offers.
